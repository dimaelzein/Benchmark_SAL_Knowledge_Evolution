Sangre de Cristo

Sa'ngre de Cristo (Sangre de Cristo), a mountain range in the southern part of the Rocky Mountains (USA, the states of Colorado and New Mexico). The length is about 300 km. Altitude up to 4363 m (mountain Blanca Peak). It is composed of red sandstones and shales. On the peaks there are glaciers and snowfields, on the slopes there are sparse mountain pine forests.
